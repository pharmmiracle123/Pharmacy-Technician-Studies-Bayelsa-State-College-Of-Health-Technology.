About the Department

The Pharmacy Technician Studies Department at Bayelsa State College of Health Technology is committed to developing skilled healthcare professionals who can support pharmacists and provide safe, effective, and ethical patient care. Our curriculum blends core pharmaceutical sciences with practical hands-on training.

Students are equipped with knowledge in pharmacology, drug dispensing, medical ethics, and hospital/community pharmacy operations. We emphasize professionalism, research, and innovation in pharmacy practice.

Graduates of the program are eligible for professional certification and are well-prepared to work in hospitals, community pharmacies, NGOs, and research institutions both locally and internationally.
